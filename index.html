Team Eight Multi-Purpose Automated Vehicle

This is the fourth year engineering project conducted in the University of Manchester. The team consists of Ekram, Daniel, Hani, Sanket, Shihab and Hussein. The aim of the team is to create a multi-purpose semi autonomous vehicle which will for the moment be used to detect land mines and to assess the health of grass.
